PT Pacific Petrol Indonesia supports fuel distribution for both private entities and national enterprises such as Pertamina and Patra Niaga.<br><br>

### **1. High Speed Diesel (HSD) / Biosolar B35**
Fuel for high-speed marine engines with 35% biofuel composition.  
Low water and sulfur content through an efficient dewatering process.

### **2. MFO 180 (HSFO 180)**
Fuel for low-speed diesel engines (<300 rpm), widely used in industrial and power plant sectors.

### **3. MFO 380 (HSFO 380)**
High-viscosity marine fuel suitable for main ship engines and industrial applications.
