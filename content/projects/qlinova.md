## Executive Summary

![Hero Image](https://ik.imagekit.io/wdujykr9f/hero-qlinova.png)<br>

Qlinova streamlines the entire clinical process, including patient registration, electronic medical records (EMR), pharmacy, inventory, billing, HR, and financial reporting. Built with scalability and integration in mind, Qlinova helps clinics transition into modern, data-driven healthcare institutions.<br><br>

The platform is engineered to deliver higher operational efficiency, reduce human errors, and provide clinicians and management with accurate and real-time information.

## Solution Overview

![Hero Image](https://ik.imagekit.io/wdujykr9f/solution-overview-qlinova.png)

Qlinova was developed as a comprehensive healthcare digitalization solution, combining **Clinic** and **Innovation**. It enables clinics to operate with modern standards using a central, secure, and intuitive digital ecosystem.<br><br>

**Key characteristics:**<br><br>

- **Integrated:** All departments operate within a unified system.  
- **Efficient:** Minimizes manual processes and reduces administrative load.  
- **Accurate:** Ensures precise and traceable data across all modules.  
- **Insightful:** Provides real-time dashboards and analytical reports.  
- **Scalable:** Ready to adapt as the clinic expands.  

## Business Flow

Qlinova supports the complete operational workflow of a clinic:<br><br>

- **Registration:** Patient data entry, queue management, contract/payment selection.  
- **Polyclinic Services:** Diagnosis entry, procedures, medications, lab/radiology referrals, treatment history.  
- **EMR Access:** Real-time medical records accessible by clinicians.  
- **Pharmacy & Inventory:** Electronic prescriptions, stock visibility, procurement, and distribution.  
- **Billing:** Cash, insurance, and corporate contracts with automated tariff calculations.  
- **Management Reporting:** End-of-day processing, financial reports, and decision-making dashboards.
